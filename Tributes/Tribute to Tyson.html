Cicely Louise Tyson

~ Just as I am ~

Here's a time line of Ms. Tyson's amazing life:

  • December 19, 1924 - She was born in the Bronx, New-York City USA
  • 1956: - First acting role in the film Carib Gold
  • 1961: - Television debut in the NBC series Frontiers of Faith
  • 1972 - she was nominated for both the Academy Award for Best Actress and Golden Globe Award for Best Actress in a Motion Picture - Drama
  • 1994 - 1995 - in the television series Sweet Justice she portrayed a civil rights activist and attorney named "Carrie Grace Battle"
  • 2005 - she had a notable film role in the drama Diary of a Mad Black Woman
  • 2014 - 2020 - In the legal serie How To Get Away With Murder she guest-starred as "Ophelia Harkness", the mother of main character Annalise Keating
  • 2020 - She starred in the popular movie A Fall From Grace
  • January 26, 2021 - Her memoir"Just as I am"was pusblished
  • January 28, 2021 - She died at the age of 96
